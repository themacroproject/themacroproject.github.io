Automate, Anything!

  • Automate repetitive tasks through the use of macros, which are sequences of keystrokes and commands that can be assigned to individual keys.
  • Rather than having to manually perform a series of actions every time you need to complete a task, you can simply press a single key or key combination to execute the entire series of actions at once.
